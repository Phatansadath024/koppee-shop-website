About Us

Rooted in Local Flavor

Our Story

From homemade pastries to cultural events—serving Mladá Boleslav with love.

Founded to bring warmth and creativity to our community, Café Srdce v Mouce blends tradition with sustainability. We host art shows, poetry, and serve fresh, homemade offerings with a human touch.

Learn More

Our Vision

To be a cultural hub where people connect over coffee, creativity, and community. We believe in local sourcing, minimal waste, and meaningful moments shared around every cup.
